## üí° TOP 10 TIPS FOR SUCCESS

### 1. **Think Out Loud**
   - Don't go silent
   - Explain your reasoning
   - Interviewer wants to see your thought process

### 2. **Ask Clarifying Questions**
   - Requirements first
   - Better to ask than assume
   - Shows thoughtful approach

### 3. **Start Simple, Then Scale**
   - MVP first
   - Then optimize
   - Explain why

### 4. **Discuss Trade-offs**
   - Every design has pros/cons
   - "It depends on..."
   - Show you understand complexity

### 5. **Use Real Numbers**
   - Makes answers concrete
   - Easier to reason about
   - Shows practical experience

### 6. **Include Monitoring**
   - Always in system design
   - Critical for production
   - Metrics, logs, traces

### 7. **Consider Costs**
   - Especially for ML systems
   - Optimization strategies
   - Business awareness

### 8. **Be Product-Minded**
   - User impact
   - Business value
   - Not just technical correctness

### 9. **Admit What You Don't Know**
   - "I'm not familiar with X..."
   - "Here's how I'd approach it..."
   - Shows honesty and problem-solving

### 10. **Stay Calm and Positive**
   - Mistakes are okay
   - Ask for hints
   - Show resilience
